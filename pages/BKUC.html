About BACHA KHAN UNIVERSITY

Bacha Khan University is a public university situated in Charsadda, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan, named after Abdul Ghaffar Khan (Bacha Khan).
The University in Charsadda was founded on 3 July 2012 with the mission to advance knowledge and learning through quality research and education for Pakistan.
Currently, the University land consists of 25 acres, with another 97 acres of land being purchased near Charsadda, Motorway Interchange. The Academic Departments at Bacha Khan University,
Charsadda currently include more than 3047 students who are enrolled in BS, Masters and MS/MPhil degree programs. There are two boys and one girls' hostel at the campus.
